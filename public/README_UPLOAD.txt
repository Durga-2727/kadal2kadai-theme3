2. Drag and drop your 10 fish images from your computer directly into this 'public' folder.
3. Make sure the files are named exactly as follows (matching your local names and prices):
   - koduva_750.jpg
   - sura_450.jpg
   - keluthi_320.jpg
   - kendai_280.jpg
   - tilapia_180.jpg
   - sheela_580.jpg
   - kanava_480.jpg
   - kalava_680.jpg
